About Me

I graduated University with a Medical Laboratory Science Degree and spent most of my career working in the Healthcare sector.

While working in a fast-paced, stressful laboratory setting dealing with potential highly infectious samples, there have always been a part of me that finds coding to be a quiet, almost soothing activity, especially when you accidentally enter into an infinite loop that you have no way to break out of =P. Coding has been part of my life whether I consciously knew it or not. From the early days of programming my own Quadratic Formula onto the TI-83 to customizing reporting rules for a laboratory analyzer to working with Macros and setting conditions on excel, the ability to code has been with me every step of my career path.

Now, it is the perfect chance for me to transition fully into the world of the Matrix... I mean Software Engineering and would love to work with all the great software engineers out there to create software that changes the way we live and improve it for a better tomorrow.
